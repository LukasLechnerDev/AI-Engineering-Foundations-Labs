# 30 - Foundation models VS ML models

## Teaching goal
- Give students a quick mental model for why LLMs feel different from traditional ML models.

## Content to learn
- traditional ML models are often trained for one narrow task
- foundation models are trained on broad data and can be reused for many tasks
- an LLM is a foundation model for language
- in this course we mainly use the model through prompting, not by training our own model

## Keep this short
- one simple contrast is enough
- do not turn this into a full ML history lesson
