## Why is there such a high demand for AI Engineers
- Historically, companies would need to train their own ML models to get "AI functionality"
- These ML models were really expensive to build, you would need to have ML Engineers in your team
- These ML models were tailored to a specific use case
- Current general-purpose foundation models can be used by companies without upfront costs, and for lots of different use cases
1. Its easier for companies to integrate foundation models (lowered the barrier for developers to integrate them)
2. Foundation models got more powerful and enabled new use cases
- So more and more companies are going to integrate AI into their products and workflows
- Companies can use "AI as a service" instead of using their own ML models
- Give you access to cutting-edge AI without needing to hire researchers
- Companies need experts - the AI Engineers - who know how to integrate AI
- high demand
- Confirmed by LinkedIn recents "Jobs on the Rise" article
