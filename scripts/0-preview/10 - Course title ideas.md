# Course title ideas

## Learning goals
- Compare course title options that are easier to find on Udemy.
- Choose a title that clearly signals job readiness and practical AI engineering skills.
- Keep the title aligned with the course MVP: building a real production-style portfolio project with Python.

## Notes
- `AI Engineer` is the strongest lead phrase because it matches the role students want.
- `Bootcamp`, `Python`, `LLM Apps`, `Production`, and `Portfolio Project` are useful supporting terms.
- `Fundamentals` feels broader and less outcome-driven than the current course direction.
- The title should promise practical outcomes, not broad AI theory.
- Current favorite: `AI Engineer Bootcamp: Build and Deploy Real LLM Apps with Python with Evaluations and Monitoring`
