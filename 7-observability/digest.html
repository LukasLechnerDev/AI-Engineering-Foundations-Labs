AI Engineer - GenAI, Creative-X

Amazon.com

Matching 58%

Description

This AI Engineer role on Amazon Advertising’s Creative X team focuses on turning generative AI research into production systems, building and deploying advertiser-facing AI agents and models at scale with applied scientists and engineers. The product is a creative generation platform for ads that uses LLMs, diffusion models, generative audio, computer vision, and reinforcement learning to create high-quality images, videos, audio, and copy for advertisers.

Highlights

  • Base salary up to $194,400 plus sign-on payments, RSUs, and comprehensive Amazon benefits (health, 401(k) match, PTO, parental leave).
  • High-impact GenAI work: build and productize AI agents, LLMs, diffusion models, computer vision, and generative audio for Amazon Ads at massive scale.
  • Strong growth/ownership opportunity: end-to-end deployment, close collaboration with top applied scientists/engineers, and chances to mentor, recruit, and present to senior leadership.

Match Analysis

  • Strong overlap in Python and LLM-related work, with 1 year building LLM-powered features and relevant backend experience that supports software development and deployment tasks.
  • Has some adjacent experience that fits model serving/deployment via FastAPI, Docker, and REST APIs, but lacks explicit evidence for several core AI specialties like agents, diffusion, CV, RL, and distributed training.
  • No clear background in data analysis, model validation, or testing/code reviews beyond general development skills, so overall fit is moderate rather than strong.
